Approach

Research-first thinking with nimble delivery.

ShenWorks combines municipal experience with boutique studio agility, allowing each project to move decisively from concept to construction documentation with the same leadership.

Context intelligence

Rapid site diagnostics, policy reviews, and stakeholder mapping built on experience inside İstanbul Municipality and IMAR Planlama.

  • Mobility and hydrology overlays
  • Climate-appropriate planting palettes
  • Feasibility & phasing road maps

Systems thinking

Landscape, architecture, and infrastructure design progress in lockstep with partners such as IDEARC, Metex Studio Erk, and MME Mimarlık.

  • Coordinated BIM + CAD packages
  • Material research & specification
  • Full planting and irrigation strategies

Delivery stewardship

Construction support and field coordination ensure that concept intent survives procurement, value engineering, and long-term care.

  • Site supervision & mock-ups
  • Operations playbooks for clients
  • Post-occupancy tuning
